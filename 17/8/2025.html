What ?

I realized that nobody is permanent in my life except myself. Life goes on without others. Today my father also told me: "দুনিয়ার কেউ কারো না। এটা সব সময় বুঝে চলবি।" Maybe the thoughts I am having today, the dreams I am chasing today—everything might be wrong. One day everyone will leave me, even my parents. Then what? Who am I? What am I really thinking? Have I ever thought deeply about it?

Why do I become so angry and rude over silly reasons? I am a human being, but maybe I have some mental issues. Why do I always want to be successful? Is being successful really that easy? I think I should be stronger in accepting my defeats. I shouldn’t get so angry if anyone says something wrong about me. They have their own tongue, so they can speak anything they want.

The way I take their words so seriously might be my own mental instability. So I should fix this. Otherwise, since nobody is permanent in my life, I should focus on myself. I know at this moment it’s very hard for me. It may take about 2 to 3 months. It will make me suffer a lot and bring indescribable mental pain. But still, I should accept it.
